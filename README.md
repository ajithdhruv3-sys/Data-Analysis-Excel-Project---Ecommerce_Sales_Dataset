# Data-Analysis-Excel-Project---Ecommerce_Sales_Dataset
# 📊 Business Performance Dashboard

This repository contains a comprehensive Power BI dashboard that visualizes key business metrics across customer demographics, product categories, regions, payment methods, and yearly revenue trends.

---

## 🚀 Overview

The dashboard provides a unified view of business performance, helping stakeholders make data‑driven decisions. It includes interactive filters for:

- Gender  
- Product Category  
- Region  
- Order Date (Year & Month)

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### **1. Gender‑Wise Loyalty Levels**
- Female customers show higher loyalty in Gold and Platinum tiers.
- Male customers are more concentrated in Bronze tier.

### **2. Category‑Wise Stock**
- Sports has the highest stock availability.
- Home, Beauty, Clothing, and Electronics maintain moderate inventory levels.

### **3. Region‑Wise Store Count**
- East and South regions lead in online store presence.
- North and South regions have the highest number of flagship stores.

### **4. Payment Method Distribution**
- PayPal: 35%  
- Credit Card: 33%  
- COD: 32%  
- All payment methods are nearly equally preferred.

### **5. Category‑Wise Quantity Sold**
- Sports and Electronics dominate sales volume.
- Beauty and Clothing contribute smaller shares.

### **6. Year‑Wise Revenue Trend**
- Strong growth from 2023 to 2024.
- Slight decline in 2025, indicating potential market or operational shifts.
